Problem statement

Engineering teams drown in unstructured bug reports.

Severity is inconsistent, owners are guessed manually, release notes are an afterthought, and P1s wait for someone to notice.

01

Manual triage

Every issue needs reading, classification, severity scoring, and context gathering.

02

Inconsistent priority

One person's P1 becomes another person's backlog item.

03

Slow ownership

The right engineer is often found by memory, not by repository evidence.

04

Weak follow-through

Incidents, acknowledgements, fixes, and release notes get split across tools.

Product

One workflow from report to action.

A report enters Cortex. Lemma agents classify and reason. Functions persist, alert, suggest owners, and prepare safe next actions.

How we built it

Lemma is the operating system for the prototype.

Agents do judgment. Functions do deterministic rules. Workflows coordinate the chain. Tables keep the source of truth.
